Cocoa

A Beloved French Brittany

2014 - 2026

Today we say goodbye to Cocoa the French Brittany.

Cocoa came to us early 2014, following my wife Stephanie's multiple organ transplants. There was a lot of sadness in our family as Stephanie had frequent hospitalizations and Cocoa was a comfort to my children during those hard times and they grew up together.

The Sweetest Dog

But despite being stubborn and always pulling on the leash when we walked her, she was one of the sweetest dogs I've ever had. She laid by my feet while I worked and became the best lap dog ever.

Memories

Last week we discovered that she had developed endocarditis, bacteria in the blood that attacks the heart valves. She quickly weakened and we knew the time had come.

We thank God for Cocoa and the joy that she brought our family.
